Our Solution

Arbiter makes the hidden structure of online campaigns visible, before the public is influenced.

  • • A journalist types one question
  • • Our AI agents collect posts from nine platforms, trace networks promoting claims across platforms, languages, and countries
  • • Every claim in the investigation links back to the original post
  • • Instead of asking you to believe them, journalists can now show you who is behind your feed
